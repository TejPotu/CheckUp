# Chronic Condition Management for Elderly

## Diabetes (Sugar Disease / షుగర్ వ్యాధి)

### Daily Monitoring
- Check fasting blood sugar: normal range 80-130 mg/dL for elderly
- Post-meal (2 hours): should be below 180 mg/dL
- HbA1c target: below 7.5% for most elderly (may be relaxed to 8% for frail elderly)

### Warning Signs
- **High sugar**: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, blurred vision, fatigue
- **Low sugar**: Sweating, trembling, confusion, hunger, irritability
- **Emergency**: Blood sugar below 54 mg/dL or above 400 mg/dL — seek immediate help

### Daily Management
- Take medications on time, with or before meals as prescribed
- Eat regular meals, do not skip
- Limit rice quantity, increase vegetable intake
- Include fiber-rich foods: whole grains, leafy vegetables
- Walk after meals for 10-15 minutes
- Regular foot care: check for cuts, wash and dry properly

## Hypertension (Blood Pressure / బీపీ)

### Normal BP for Elderly
- Target: below 140/90 mmHg (may be relaxed to 150/90 for those over 80)
- Check BP at least once a week at the same time

### Warning Signs of High BP
- Severe headache, especially at the back of the head
- Blurred vision or seeing spots
- Chest pain or tightness
- Nosebleeds
- Shortness of breath
- **Emergency**: BP above 180/120 — seek immediate help

### Daily Management
- Take BP medications at the same time every day
- Reduce salt intake (max 1 teaspoon per day)
- Avoid pickles, papads, and processed foods
- Regular walking and light exercise
- Manage stress through prayer, meditation, or hobbies
- Limit alcohol, avoid smoking

## Arthritis (Joint Pain / కీళ్ల నొప్పులు)

### Types Common in Elderly
- **Osteoarthritis**: Wear-and-tear of joints — common in knees, hips, hands
- **Rheumatoid arthritis**: Inflammatory — stiffness especially in mornings

### Daily Management
- Gentle exercises: walking, swimming, range-of-motion exercises
- Apply warm compress for stiffness, cold compress for swelling
- Maintain healthy weight to reduce joint load
- Use assistive devices (walking stick, grab bars) if needed
- Anti-inflammatory foods: turmeric, ginger, omega-3 (fish oil)
- Avoid sitting cross-legged or on the floor for long periods

### When to See Doctor
- Pain that wakes you from sleep
- Joint swelling with redness and warmth
- Inability to move a joint
- Pain not relieved by usual medications

## Heart Disease (గుండె జబ్బు)

### Warning Signs of Heart Attack
- Chest pain or pressure (may feel like heaviness or squeezing)
- Pain spreading to left arm, jaw, or back
- Shortness of breath
- Cold sweats, nausea
- Unusual fatigue
- **ANY of these symptoms → Call 108 immediately**

### Daily Heart Health
- Take all heart medications as prescribed — never skip
- Low-salt, low-fat diet
- Walk 20-30 minutes daily if cleared by doctor
- Monitor weight — sudden weight gain may indicate fluid retention
- Report any new or worsening shortness of breath
- Annual heart check-up recommended
